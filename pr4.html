Artificial Intelligence (AI)
Is technology enabling machines to simulate human intelligence, learning, problem-solving and perception through data analysis, allowing them to perform tasks like understanding language,recognizing images, making decisions, and even creating content, as seen in virtual assistants (Siri, Alexa), recommendation systems (Netflix, Amazon), and autonomous vehicles. It works by processing vast amounts of data using techniques like machine learning and deep learning, enabling systems to improve autonomously and find patterns,automating complex processes across industries from healthcare to finance.
